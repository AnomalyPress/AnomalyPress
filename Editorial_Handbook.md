# Anomaly Press — Editorial Handbook v1.0

## 1. Purpose

This handbook defines the operational workflow of Anomaly Press.

It translates the Editorial Charter into a practical system for producing, structuring, reviewing, and publishing analytical work.

It governs *how writing is made*, not what the publication believes.

---

## 2. Editorial System Overview

Anomaly Press operates as a structured publication pipeline with three core layers:

### 2.1 Structural Layer (Book Logic)
Defines the conceptual spine and cumulative argument of a publication series.

### 2.2 Production Layer (Writing Output)
Generates individual articles and analytical pieces.

### 2.3 Synthesis Layer (Book Formation)
Combines structured articles into long-form publications.

---

## 3. Core Production Principle

> Every publication must serve a structural role within a larger interpretive system.

No article exists in isolation. Every output must contribute to a cumulative analytical architecture.

---

## 4. Book-Driven Publishing Model

Anomaly Press uses a book-first accumulation model:

- Articles are nodes in a structured argument
- A sequence of articles forms a thematic spine
- The spine resolves into a book-length work

### Book 1 Structure Principle:
All Book 1 outputs must map to the canonical spine:

> Detection is insufficient → prediction collapses → information overload degrades clarity → interpretive frameworks fail → interpretation must be constrained and controlled → systems are non-stationary → humans over-explain → therefore interpretation must become a disciplined practice.

---

## 5. Article Development Workflow

Each article follows a controlled production pipeline:

### Step 1 — Spine Mapping
Identify which transition in the Book 1 spine the article addresses.

### Step 2 — Functional Role Assignment
Define the article’s role:

- Expansion of a spine node  
- Transition explanation between nodes  
- Failure mode analysis  
- Methodological or interpretive response  

### Step 3 — Structural Construction
Build the article using layered reasoning rather than narrative flow.

### Step 4 — Internal Coherence Check
Ensure:
- Logical consistency  
- Separation of observation vs interpretation  
- Structural alignment with spine  

### Step 5 — Editorial Stabilisation
Confirm:
- No topic drift  
- No unsupported conclusions  
- Controlled ambiguity where required  

---

## 6. Writing Standards

All Anomaly Press outputs must adhere to:

- Structural clarity over stylistic embellishment  
- Analytical reasoning over narrative expansion  
- Controlled interpretation over speculation  
- Separation of observation and inference  
- Resistance to premature closure of ideas  

---

## 7. Acceptable Output Formats

The following formats are permitted:

- Editorial articles  
- Analytical essays  
- Research papers  
- White papers  
- Structured theoretical frameworks  
- Book-length series  
- Interview-based analytical discussions  
- Curated collections  

---

## 8. Drift Control Rules

Content must be excluded or revised if it:

- Introduces unrelated thematic expansion  
- Breaks structural alignment with Book 1 spine  
- Functions as standalone commentary  
- Lacks analytical or interpretive structure  
- Prioritises narrative over system coherence  

---

## 9. Publishing Pipeline

### Standard Flow:

Idea → Spine Mapping → Role Assignment → Drafting → Structural Review → Publication

---

## 10. Version Control Logic

- Articles are immutable once published unless structurally revised
- Structural changes require re-mapping to the Book 1 spine
- Book synthesis is a later-stage operation, not a live editing process

---

## 11. Synthesis Stage (Book Formation)

When sufficient articles exist:

- Articles are reorganised into book chapters  
- Redundant material is removed  
- Structural transitions are refined  
- A coherent narrative spine is constructed from the analytical sequence  

Books are *emergent structures*, not pre-written documents.

---

## 12. Editorial Discipline Principle

Anomaly Press prioritises:

> coherence over volume, structure over output, and interpretation discipline over narrative clarity.

---

## 13. Closing Statement

This handbook governs how Anomaly Press produces work.

It ensures that every publication contributes to a cumulative, structured, and disciplined body of analytical knowledge rather than isolated commentary.
